## Typography
- **Font Family:** Rethink Sans (variable font, wght axis)
- **Files:** `RethinkSans-VariableFont_wght.ttf`, `RethinkSans-Italic-VariableFont_wght.ttf`
- **Weights available:** Regular (400), Medium (500), SemiBold (600), Bold (700), ExtraBold (800)
- **Border Radius (buttons):** 8px standard, 12px soft variant
